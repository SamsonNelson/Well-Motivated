Well-Motivated

Keynote

We provide speaking services to large conferences in need of keynote speakers and / or break-out session facilitators. We also cater to smaller groups who may be looking for a speaker to kick off a company meeting in a fresh, new way. Our keynote speeches are usually delivered from the stage or a podium and typically run about one hour in length, but can be expanded or contracted depending on your group's needs. We incorporate the use of fresh visuals, humor, and illustrative video clips (time-permitting).

Workshops

For many people, attending a Well-Motivated Seminar is a life changing event because it creates a paradigm shift in how they perceive and interact with other people. The seminar presents information that is applicable in any setting - personal or professional. The course content is, above all, realistic and practical. Participants work on the organization's real issues and develop action plans and receive materials to use on the job the very next day.

Coaching

We will help you understand how others perceive you and will help coach and how to play to your strengths rather than fall into patterns that limit your success or damage your reputation. This knowledge can better prepare you for career advancement opportunities now and far into the future. We will teach you team building, the importance of EQ in the work place equation, and help you develop character in yourself and your team.
